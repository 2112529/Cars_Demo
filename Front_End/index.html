About this project

项目介绍

The case aims to use New York car data for in-depth data analysis and visualization to reveal valuable information related to New York City cars. The dataset includes multiple fields covering the car's status, name, price, exterior color, interior color, drive style, fuel efficiency, fuel type, transmission, engine, mileage, convenience, entertainment, appearance, safety Features, Seats, Cleaning Title, Make, Year, Model, Overall Rating, Comfort Score, Design Score, Value Score, Style Score, and Reliability Score.

Through the analysis and visual display of these data, the analysis obtains:

Automobile market trends: Through the analysis of New York car makes, years and models, we can understand the status and changing trends of the local auto market. We can discover popular brands, popular years, and consumer preferences for different models.

Car Value and Style: By analyzing factors such as car price, value-for-money score, and design score, we can assess the value and style of cars in New York City. This can help consumers consider the balance between price and performance when buying a car, and it also provides automakers with suggestions for improving their products.
